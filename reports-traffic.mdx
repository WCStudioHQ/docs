# 🚦 Traffic & Conversion Reports

The **Traffic Reports** section in WC Studio gives you real-time visibility into how visitors are finding, navigating, and interacting with your store. Combined with conversion data, this helps you understand which sources drive results and where users drop off.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Reports → Traffic

---

## 📈 What You Can Track

### 🌐 Traffic Sources

- Organic Search (Google, Bing, etc.)
- Direct traffic
- Referral websites
- Social media platforms
- Paid Ads (if UTM tags are in place)

> View source-based performance like bounce rate, average time on site, and conversion contribution.

---

### 📍 Top Landing Pages

- See which pages attract the most visitors
- Analyze landing page engagement (bounce, exit, scroll depth)
- Use this to optimize your homepage, category pages, or campaign-specific URLs

---

### 📊 Conversion Metrics

- Total sessions vs total conversions
- Conversion rate by traffic source
- Abandonment rate (cart and checkout)
- Time to conversion

---

## 🔍 Behavior Flow

Visualize how users move through your store:

- Entry page → category/product → cart → checkout → thank you
- Identify where most users drop off or bounce
- Improve key touchpoints (like cart or shipping options)

---

## 📤 Exporting Reports

Export traffic and conversion data as:

- CSV
- Excel
- PDF

Helpful for campaign reviews, team meetings, or client performance reports.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- ✅ Add UTM tags to every ad and campaign URL to track source-level results
- ✅ Monitor drop-off points in the behavior flow to improve user experience
- ✅ Use high-performing landing pages as templates for new promotions

# 📦 Order Reports & Customer Insights

Tracking orders and understanding your customers is essential to running a healthy eCommerce business. 

WC Studio’s **Order Reports** give you real-time visibility into how customers interact with your store — from the moment they place an order to how often they return.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Reports → Orders

---

## 📋 What You Can Monitor

### ✅ Order Activity Overview

- Total number of orders
- Completed, pending, failed, refunded orders
- Filter by:
  - Date range
  - Order status
  - Product/category

### 📅 Orders by Time

- View order trends over time
- Spot buying patterns (e.g., weekends, holidays)
- Compare performance across weeks or months

### 📍 Orders by Location

- See where your orders are coming from
- Heatmaps and charts by:
  - Country
  - Region
  - City

---

## 🧑‍💼 Customer Insights

### 🧠 Repeat Customer Tracking

- Identify repeat buyers vs first-time customers
- Measure customer retention and loyalty
- Spot high-value customers

### 🛍 Customer Purchase Behavior

- Average orders per customer
- Frequency of purchases
- Commonly bought-together products

---

## 📤 Exporting Order Reports

You can export order and customer insights as:

- CSV
- PDF
- Excel

Perfect for sharing with teams, clients, or stakeholders.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- ✅ Use customer insights to build segments for email marketing
- ✅ Identify high-spending customers and target them with VIP perks
- ✅ Use low-conversion regions to tweak your shipping or localization strategies

# ⚙️ General Settings – Team Collaboration

The **General Settings** section under WC Studio’s collaboration panel allows you to manage your team identity and ownership information. This is especially helpful for agencies or companies working on multiple stores with shared resources.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Collaborators → General

---

## 🧾 Team Information

| Field         | Description                                          |
|---------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| **Team Owner**| The current owner/admin of the WC Studio account     |
| **Team Email**| The primary email used for account and billing       |
| **Team Name** | Your team or agency name as it appears in the system |

---

### ✏️ Editing Team Name

To personalize your workspace or reflect your brand:

1. Navigate to **General Settings**
2. Update the **Team Name** field
3. Click **Save** to apply changes

✅ The team name is used across dashboards, store ownership labels, and collaborator invitations.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- ✅ Use your **agency or business name** for better brand consistency
- ✅ Make sure your **Team Email** is up-to-date for billing & alerts
- ✅ Only the **Team Owner** can edit these settings

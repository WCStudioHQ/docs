# 📦 Product Performance & Inventory Reports

The **Products** section inside WC Studio Reports gives you deep insight into how your products are performing — from top-sellers and low inventory to product trends and revenue per item. Use this data to optimize pricing, promotion, and stock decisions.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Reports → Products

---

## 📊 What You Can Track

### 🛍️ Product Performance Overview

- Total products sold (unit and revenue-based)
- Top-performing products by:
  - Quantity sold
  - Revenue generated
  - Conversion rate

### 💸 Revenue Per Product

- View revenue contribution per item
- Spot high-margin vs low-margin products
- Analyze seasonal or trending products

---

## 🧾 Inventory Monitoring

### 📉 Low Stock Alerts

- Get notified when products are below the restock threshold
- Track fast-moving SKUs before they go out of stock

### 🚫 Out-of-Stock Items

- See a list of products currently unavailable
- Prioritize restocking for high-demand items

### 📦 Stock Value Overview

- Total value of current inventory
- Breakdown by category or product type

---

## 🔄 Product Trends & Movement

- Compare product performance over time
- Discover sales trends by season or marketing push
- Identify stagnating items for clearance or promotion

---

## 📤 Exporting Product Reports

Download detailed product performance reports in:
- CSV
- Excel
- PDF

Useful for inventory planning, marketing strategy, or client reporting.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- ✅ Use inventory data to plan ahead for sales campaigns
- ✅ Track which products bring in the most repeat buyers
- ✅ Combine product reports with customer insights to identify power combinations

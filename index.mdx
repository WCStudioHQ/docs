## ⚙️ Basic Requirements

Before you create your first store, make sure you have:

- ✅ A registered **WC Studio account** – [Sign up here](https://my.wcstudio.com/register)
- ✅ After registration, Sign in to WC Studio
- ✅ Select a plan that suits your need
- ✅ Welcome to the dashboard of WC Studio
---

# 🛠️ Creating Your First Store

Follow these step-by-step instructions to launch your store in just a few clicks.

---

## ✅ Step 1: Start Store Creation

1. From the dashboard, click **“Create New Store”**
2. Enter your **Store Name** (e.g., “Trendify Fashion”)
3. Choose a **subdomain** (e.g., `trendify.wcstudio.com`)
4. Select your **Business Type**:
   - 🛍️ Retail Store
   - 🏪 Wholesale Store
   - 📥 Digital Products
   - 🛒 Marketplace

---

## ✅ Step 2: Choose a Template

Choose how you'd like to set up your store:

- 🎨 **Use a Pre-Built Template** (e.g., Fashion, Tech, Electronics) for instant layout and design
- ⚙️ **Start from Scratch** with a blank WooCommerce installation for full customization

---


## ✅ Step 3: Launch Your Store

Click **“Create Store”** — WC Studio will:

- 🔧 Automatically install WooCommerce and configure core settings
- 🛠 Provision your environment (Live, Staging, or Development)
- 🔗 Connect your subdomain or custom domain
- 📦 Redirect you to the **WC Studio Admin Panel**

🎉 **Congratulations!** Your store is now ready for further usecases.

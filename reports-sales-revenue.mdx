# 💰 Sales & Revenue Reports

Understanding your store’s sales performance is key to growth. WC Studio’s **Sales Reports** provide clear insights into total revenue, order activity, and customer spending behavior — all in one place.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Reports → Sales

---

## 📊 What You Can Track

### ✅ Total Revenue

- View gross and net revenue across:
  - Daily
  - Weekly
  - Monthly
  - Custom date ranges

### ✅ Sales Trends

- Visual charts showing:
  - Peaks and dips in sales volume
  - Seasonal patterns and campaigns
  - Growth trends over time

### ✅ Average Order Value (AOV)

- Understand how much customers spend on average
- Track improvements after applying offers, upsells, or bundles

---

## 🔍 Key Metrics at a Glance

| Metric                 | Description                                           |
|------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| **Total Revenue**      | Total amount earned in the selected time period       |
| **Total Orders**       | Number of orders completed during the period          |
| **Average Order Value**| Average revenue per order                             |
| **Top Products**       | Highest-selling products by revenue or quantity       |
| **Sales by Category**  | Breakdown of revenue by product category              |

---

## 📤 Exporting Reports

Click the **“Export”** button to download your sales data as:
- CSV
- PDF
- Excel

Perfect for team reports, presentations, or investor updates.

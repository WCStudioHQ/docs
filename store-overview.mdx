# 🛠️ Navigating the Store Management System

The **Store Management System** in WC Studio gives you full control over each store you create — from domain settings to backups and advanced configurations. This section walks you through how to access and understand the store overview.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Stores → Select a Store

Once you select a store from your list, you’ll be taken to its **dedicated store management dashboard**. Here, you’ll find everything you need to monitor, modify, and maintain your store.

---

## 🔎 Store Overview

At the bottom of the store panel, you’ll find a complete overview of your selected site.

### ✅ Site Details
- **Site Title** – The store’s public-facing name.
- **Description** – Internal description for identification.
- **Status** – Live, Offline, or In Progress.
- **Created At** – Timestamp of store creation.
- **Created By** – The WC Studio user who created the store.
- **PHP Version** – The PHP runtime powering your store.
- **WordPress Version** – Your store’s current WP core version.

---

### ✅ Web Directory & Environment

- **Web Directory** – The internal path to your store instance.
- **Environment Type** – Shows whether the store is in:
  - `Live`
  - `Development`
  - `Staging`

💡 **Pro Tip:** Quickly verify the store’s health by checking if the **WP & PHP versions** are up-to-date in this panel.

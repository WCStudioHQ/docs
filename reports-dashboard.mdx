# 📊 Navigating the Reports Dashboard

WC Studio gives you real-time insights into your store’s performance, sales trends, and business activity — all from a single, intuitive report interface.

---

## 📍 Go to: WC Studio → Reports

---

## 📈 What You’ll See on the Dashboard

The Reports Dashboard is designed to provide a high-level overview of your store’s key performance indicators (KPIs), including:

### 🛒 Store Overview

- **Total Orders** – Completed, pending, refunded
- **Revenue Overview** – Daily, weekly, monthly, and custom range
- **Average Order Value** – How much your customers typically spend per order
- **Total Customers** – Active vs new customers over time

---

### 🕵️‍♂️ Sales Insights

- Top-performing products by revenue
- Best-selling categories
- Sales by country, channel, or campaign
- Repeat purchase behavior

---

### 📦 Inventory & Product Metrics

- Low stock alerts
- Out-of-stock products
- SKU performance
- Revenue per product

---

### 💰 Refund & Return Analytics

- Refund totals by product or category
- Return rate percentage
- Common refund reasons (if tracked via plugins)

---

### 🔎 Filter & Customize Your View

Use the filters and date ranges to customize what you see:

| Filter Type        | Description                                  |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Date Range**     | View reports by day, week, month, or custom  |
| **Store**          | Select a specific store (if you manage many) |
| **Product/Category**| Drill down into specific product insights   |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- ✅ Use filters to find **sales anomalies** or seasonality trends
- ✅ Combine this data with your **marketing analytics** for deeper insight
- ✅ Export reports for stakeholder or client presentations

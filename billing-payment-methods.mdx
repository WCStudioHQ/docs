# 💳 Payment Methods – Add & Manage Your Billing Info

📍 Go to: WC Studio → Billing → My Plan → Payment Methods

The **Payment Methods** section allows you to securely manage how you pay for your WC Studio subscription. You can add new cards, update expired ones, or switch between multiple payment options.

---

## 🔐 How Payments Work in WC Studio

- Subscriptions renew automatically using your primary saved payment method
- You can use major credit/debit cards or PayPal (if supported in your region)
- WC Studio encrypts and stores your data securely using a PCI-compliant gateway

---

## ➕ Adding a New Payment Method

1. Go to **Billing → Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter your card details (or connect PayPal if available)
4. Click **Save**

✅ Your card will now be used for upcoming invoices and renewals.

---

## 🔁 Updating or Replacing a Card

- Click **Update Card**
- Enter the new payment information
- Save your changes

💡 If your previous card expired or failed, your subscription may pause until a valid payment method is added.

---

## 📋 Payment Method Table

| Field         | Description                      |
|---------------|----------------------------------|
| **Card Type** | (e.g., Visa, MasterCard)         |
| **Last 4 Digits** | For secure identification    |
| **Status**    | Active / Expired / Default       |
| **Action**    | Update / Remove                  |

You can only have **one active card** as the primary payment method at a time.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- ✅ Always keep a valid payment method on file to avoid service interruptions
- ✅ If you manage multiple stores, use a business card to keep expenses centralized
- ✅ WC Studio never shares or exposes full card numbers
